Our Story

A Little Piece of Home

Welcome to Cozy Corner Cafe, your neighborhood gathering spot where friends become family! Since 2010, we've been serving up delicious coffee, homemade pastries, and hearty comfort food with a side of warm hospitality.

Started by the Martinez family, our cafe was born from a simple dream: create a space where everyone feels welcome. Whether you're grabbing a quick latte on your morning commute, meeting friends for brunch, or bringing the kids for hot chocolate and cookies, we've got a cozy spot just for you!
