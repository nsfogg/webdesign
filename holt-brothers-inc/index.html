Our Story

From The Field To Building Dreams

After successful careers in professional football, brothers Terrence and Torry Holt returned to their roots with a vision to make a lasting impact beyond the field.

Founded in 2007, Holt Brothers Inc. has grown from a bold vision into an award-winning construction and development company. What started as a commitment to excellence on the football field has transformed into a dedication to building exceptional projects that enhance and uplift communities.

Through strategic partnerships and independent project pursuits, we've managed projects ranging from $500,000 to $450 million, establishing ourselves as a trusted name in construction management at risk projects across the Southeast.
